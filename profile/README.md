# Livya

Modern software for the real estate industry.

We build tools that help **real estate developers**, brokers, property managers, and buyers explore real estate projects through fast, interactive, and data-driven experiences.

---

## What we build

Our platform powers digital experiences across multiple real estate segments, including residential, commercial, industrial, office, and event spaces—with the flexibility to expand into new markets as the platform evolves.

Our solutions include:

- 🏗️ Project experiences
- 🏢 Building & property presentations
- 🏘️ Unit inventory & availability
- 🗺️ Interactive maps
- 📐 Floor plans
- 📷 Galleries & virtual tours
- 📊 Analytics & insights
- ⚡ Embeddable experiences for any website
